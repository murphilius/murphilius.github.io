Interests + Tech

I like to spend my time playing video games with my friends. My time spent with my friends is usually us going out to lunch, going on a trip to Trader's World, or going to the local gaming cafe, Vyral, to get a look at new pc equipment.

My friends and I like to go out to eat, and a couple of the places that have caught our eye is Raising Canes, Piada, Chipotle, and even a simple McDonald's meal.

The video games my friends and I play consists of Counter-Strike, Deep Rock Galactic, Minecraft, Team Fortress 2, Hunt Showdown, and Left 4 Dead 2. A solo game that I find both cathartic and nostalgic is Call of Duty Zombies.

I could ramble for days about my countless hours when I was kid playing Call of Duty zombies, about how it's nice to let loose and zone out for an hour or two, mindlessly moving wave through wave and navigating the intricate easter eggs the game has to offer.

If I had to pick my favorite game of all time, it would easily be the classic experience, Call of Duty: Black Ops 1.
